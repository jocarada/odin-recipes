Fried Chickpeas in Coconut Milk

Chickpeas are fried to a golden crunch with garlic, onions, ginger and spices before being simmered with coconut milk and greens

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 white onion
  • 1 tablespoon diced ginger
  • 1 tablespoon turmeric
  • 2 teaspoons berebere spice mix
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • Greens, such as kale, or mustard or collared greens

Recipe

  1. Heat oil in a cast iron skillet until glossy
  2. Add garlic, onion, and ginger and saute fro 30 seconds
  3. Add chickpeas and continue frying until chickpeas start to darken and develop a crunchy exterior
  4. Stir in turmeric and berebere and continue to saute for 30 seconds
  5. Add coconut milk, tomato paste, greens, and broth and simmer for 15 minutes, until sauce begins to thicken and chickpeas are soft
  6. Serve with rice or couscous
